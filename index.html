About Me

Hi, I’m Danny O. Brenio, a developer who genuinely loves backend development. I enjoy solving problems that most users never see—database logic, server-side architecture, system behavior, and performance optimization. I’m especially interested in how software works at a deeper level, from operating systems to backend frameworks and server configuration. I’m constantly learning, experimenting, and improving my skills by building real projects and breaking things until I understand them.

What drives me:

  • Writing clean and logical backend code
  • Understanding how systems work under the hood
  • Turning ideas into functional, working systems
